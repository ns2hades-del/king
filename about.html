Our Story

VICTH was founded on April 13, 2026, by a visionary team of developers united by a single mission: to revolutionize digital payments in Africa.

Led by Melkisédeck DJIMADJO, our collective of talented developers pooled their expertise and passion to create a fintech solution that is not just innovative, but deeply rooted in understanding African market needs.

From day one, we understood that Africa needed a fintech platform that combines cutting-edge technology with simplicity, security, and affordability. VICTH is the answer to that call.
